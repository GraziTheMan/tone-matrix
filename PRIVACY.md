# Tone Matrix — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 18 July 2026_

Tone Matrix is a music sequencer app. It is designed to work entirely on
your device.

## Data collection

Tone Matrix does **not** collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no
network communication of any kind initiated by the app.

## Data storage

Your patterns and settings are saved locally on your device only, so the
app can restore them when you reopen it. They never leave your device
unless you explicitly export a file and choose to share it.

## Files you export

When you use "Export MIDI" or "Export WAV", the app creates a file and
opens the Android share sheet so **you** decide where it goes (your files,
cloud storage, another app). The app itself does not send the file
anywhere.

## Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions. If a MIDI output device feature
is used in a browser context, device access is mediated by the browser's
own permission prompt.

## Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue at
